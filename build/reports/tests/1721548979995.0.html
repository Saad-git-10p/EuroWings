Privacy settings

We use cookies and similar technologies which are required for operating the app and the website. You can also give your consent to the following processing purposes by clicking on the I understand button.

  • Analysis, access to and storage of information on your device. With the help of analysis cookies, we can analyse our performance to give you a better online experience and evaluate the efficiency of our campaigns.

  • Personalisation cookies allow you to access a custom experience on our website with usage-dependent offers and support.

  • Ultimately, marketing cookies are issued by third-party companies that use your data to produce target group lists in order to deliver targeted advertising on social media and online.

You are free to decide whether to give consent or withdraw it at any time by clicking on the Change cookie settings link at the bottom of each page. You can select your preferred processing options by going to My settings and confirming these by clicking on Save selection. For more information, including information about processing by third-party providers, please read our data protection and cookie Information.

Flight Status

Get the latest flight times instantly

To find out about the status of a Eurowings flight, you have two query options: entering a flight number or a flight route. All times are local. A flight status is only available for Eurowings flights and flight combinations with at least one Eurowings flight. 

Please note that although your flight may be delayed, you must still arrive at check-in and the departure gate on time as scheduled. We ask all passengers to check the status of their flight regularly.

Please note: If your Eurowings flight is affected by a cancellation, you have the following three options online, both before and after your scheduled departure time:

  • Rebook onto an alternative Eurowings flight free of charge

  • Cancel the flight and exchange it for a flight voucher or

  • Cancel the flight and request a refund.

Cancelled flights can also be rebooked or cancelled via the Eurowings iOS app or Android app.

Entry regulations for passengers
